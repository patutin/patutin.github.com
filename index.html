About

I'm a Principal Software Engineer at EPAM Systems, based in Batumi, Georgia. My focus today is on agentic AI systems and AI-augmented engineering: I derived an AI Adoption program for a 2,000-FTE customer, built an AI Migration Factory pipeline for moving legacy applications forward with AI, and shipped reusable AI Agent Skills adopted organization-wide.

Alongside delivery, I run internal courses on practical AI application development (LangChain, Semantic Kernel) and have championed GitHub Copilot adoption with certification and onboarding programs. The platforms I go deep on right now — LangChain, Semantic Kernel, and Anthropic's Claude Code.

Before AI took center stage, most of my career was spent on the .NET platform — distributed services, performance, and developer tooling. That foundation in production systems is what I bring to every AI initiative: shipping things that actually run, not just demos.
